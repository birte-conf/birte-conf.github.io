Thirteenth International Workshop on
Real-Time Business Intelligence and Analytics

August 26, 2019 - Los Angeles, CA, USA



Home

The Thirteenth International Workshop on Real-Time Business Intelligence and Analytics (BIRTE 2019) will be held on August 26, 2019 in conjunction with the VLDB 2019 Conference that will take place from August 26 to 30, 2019 at Los Angeles, CA, USA.

Following the previous years' workshops, the BIRTE 2019 workshop aims at providing a forum for presentation of the latest research results, new technology developments, and new applications in the areas of business intelligence and real time enterprise. Its proceedings will be published by ACM (ICPS).
